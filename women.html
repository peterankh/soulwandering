The Details

A meditative walk exclusively for women. Because sometimes we simply want to walk amongst ourselves, for endless reasons – or perhaps none at all

The walk

Travelled for the centuries, el Camino spans over nine hundred kilometers across northern Spain, from the Pyrenees to Santiago. Our journey starts right in Pamplona (for travelers coming from Barcelona, we can also arrange the Barcelona-Pamplona transfer).
After a brief introduction to our trip, in the afternoon we have a guided tour of this town known for its San Fermin bull runs, which Hemingway wrote about in his books. Following a wine-tasting experience, you will be free to explore Pamplona's narrow streets, elegant boutiques and top tapas bars.
The next morning we start walking, staying overnight in small villages that have retained their medieval charm.
In Logroño we have time to recover from these first hikes with a free half-day to devote to art, cuisine, shopping or simply doing nothing, before plunging back into the astonishing wine landscapes of La Rioja, where we stay in a wonderful hotel-bodega that will offer us spa, wine tasting and a guided tour to discover the wine making process.
After the last few days of walking, we will celebrate the end of this first section in the unspoilt splendour of Santo Domingo de la Calzada, where we will lodge in a luxury inn nestled inside a renovated ancient monastery. Hoping to see you again for the next leg!
This walk will be guided by our Audrey. Hold tight: she is also a Shiatsu therapist and massage expert. And at the end of the day she will be available for individual sessions upon request (please, not all together on the same afternoon! :))

A typical day

We start walking at around 8:30 am, after breakfast. Covering around 20 km daily, with a maximum of 24 km, we prioritize a leisurely pace, allowing everyone to walk at their own rhythm.
Weather permitting, lunch will normally be a gourmet picnic, in some quiet natural space along the way. We end our daily walk between 4 and 6 pm. Before having dinner together, there is time to rest, explore the surroundings or enjoy a glass of wine.
Every evening a culinary adventure awaits us to discover the cuisine and local products of the best restaurants that we will find along the way.

The lodging

We always stay in beautiful accommodation - boutique hotels, rural guesthouses, luxury inns in renovated ancient monasteries. Each establishment has been carefully selected to offer you the best.
Our standard, aimed at strengthening the group spirit, is to offer shared rooms with twin beds. If you are travelling as a couple and prefer a double bed, you can specify this when making your request.
Single rooms may be available in some accommodations upon request. A single occupancy supplement will apply.

Escape Velocity

What is this meditative walking?

Let us be clear. This is not a retreat, and we are not gurus. Not that we don't like retreats. Simply, our walks are an immersive practice: wandering with mindfulness through pleasurable sensory experiences. As facilitators, we will offer daily meditation tips to help you enjoy the journey in a more mindful way.

Yes, you can!

You will walk at your own pace, unhurriedly, according to the rhythms of your body. And we will stop repeatedly at agreed places to have a coffee in a charming stone village or to enjoy the beauty of the landscape. Our walks are open to people of all ages in reasonably good physical condition.

With yourself, in a group

Our itinerary balances solitude and togetherness. We encourage moments of silence, digital disconnection and introspection. But our walks include also powerful experiences as a group, such as shared meals, guided tours, evening conversations in front of a drink and much more.

The delights of comfort

In the spirit of SoulWandering, no sacrifices are expected. We always sleep in beautiful rural guesthouses or boutique hotels. Every evening a culinary adventure awaits us to discover the best local cuisine, to regain our strength and delight our palates.
